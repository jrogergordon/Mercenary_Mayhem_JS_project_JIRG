• For The King!
    a game by Jacob Irving Roger-Gordon

    Use the arrow keys and spacebar to select a character and move around.

    Keep the King safe and kill the assassin!

    Hello brave sir knight! Earlier you were tasked with guarding the King, and just your luck now an assassin has appeared! Use the on screen cursor along with the arrow keys to move around the screen. Use the spacebar to select a character (either the King or the guard Boyd) and move them around. If Boyd touches the assassin he will damage him. If he damages him twice the assassin will die! But beware! If the assassin touches the king even once he will kill the king and you will lose! The King and Boyd can move 6 spaces each, after each time you move either character the assassin, Petrine, will move 4 spaces.             Good Luck!
    Instructions
    • Move around the King and Knight
    • Keep the king away from the assassin
    • Touch the assassin with the knight to damage him
    • Damage the assassin twice to win!
